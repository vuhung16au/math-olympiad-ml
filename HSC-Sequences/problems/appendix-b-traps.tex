\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Mixing up term formulas and sum formulas:
  $a_n$ and $S_n$ answer different questions.

  \item Losing track of index starts:
  check whether the sequence starts at $n=0$ or $n=1$.

  \item Using $S_\infty=\frac{a}{1-r}$ when $|r|\ge1$:
  this formula only applies for $|r|<1$.

  \item Sign errors in GP sums:
  keep the exact formula form and substitute carefully.

  \item Assuming recursion converges without proof:
  identify monotonicity or contraction before claiming limits.

  \item Treating recurring decimals as finite truncations:
  they are infinite geometric expansions.
\end{itemize}
