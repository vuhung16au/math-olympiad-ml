
## What this is

I maintain a set of **HSC Mathematics Extension 2** booklets that cover a wide range of core and supporting topics. The materials are designed for **students and teachers** who want clear, topic-focused write-ups they can work through, extend, and share in class or for self-study.

Everything is published under **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)**. You are free to use, copy, adapt, and redistribute the work, including for commercial use, as long as you **give appropriate credit**, provide a link to the licence, and indicate if you made changes.

## Read in the browser (recommended)

**HSC Math Hub (booklet viewer):** [https://hsc-math-hub.vercel.app/](https://hsc-math-hub.vercel.app/)

That site is the main entry point. From one page you can **browse the full set**, open any booklet in an **in-browser PDF viewer** with the usual controls (page navigation, zoom, download, print), and see every title in one list. If you only share a single link with your cohort, use this one: students do not have to hunt through separate threads to find a matching PDF.

Alternatively, you can open the booklets from [the github releases](https://github.com/vuhung16au/math-olympiad-ml): https://github.com/vuhung16au/math-olympiad-ml

## How to use the materials

- **Students:** Use the hub to open a topic, read on screen, or download for offline work. Cross-link between topics is easier when all titles are listed in one place.
- **Teachers:** You can point the class to the hub, extract sections you need for a unit, and adapt or supplement under CC BY 4.0 as long as you credit the source as required by the licence.

## Version and year

This write-up and the associated uploads are aimed at the **2026** HSC cycle (and ongoing revision use). Replace the year in short blurbs if you refresh the pack for a later cohort.
