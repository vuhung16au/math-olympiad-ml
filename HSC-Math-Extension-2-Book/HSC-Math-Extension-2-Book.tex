\chapter*{Preface}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Preface}
This volume brings together the existing HSC Mathematics Extension 2 booklets into a single reference text. It is designed as a practical study companion: core syllabus topics come first, enrichment topics follow, then a curated problem collection, and finally the most demanding ``last resort'' problems.

\medskip
The goal of this combined book is not to replace the original topic booklets, but to make them easier to study as one continuous resource. The original booklet content is reused as much as possible so each chapter preserves the style, pacing, and mathematical detail of its source.

\medskip
Students can read this book in sequence, or jump directly to a part that matches their current revision focus. Teachers and tutors may also use the chapter boundaries to assign topic-specific practice while still keeping the wider Extension 2 landscape in view.
