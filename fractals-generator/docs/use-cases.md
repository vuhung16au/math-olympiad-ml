# Fractal Use Cases

Related docs:
- `docs/architecture.md`
- `docs/techstack.md`
- `README.md`

This document lists major real-world applications for the most famous fractal families in this project.

## IFS

- Plant-like procedural modeling in education and graphics
- Texture synthesis and natural shape approximation
- Compression and iterative geometric modeling examples

## L-system

- Botanical growth simulation
- Rule-based grammar teaching for STEM classes
- Procedural content generation in games

## Escape-time

- Complex dynamics visualization (Mandelbrot/Julia)
- Color mapping and numerical methods demonstrations
- Zoom-based computational art

## Newton

- Numerical analysis and root-finding visualization
- Basin-of-attraction teaching material
- Iterative method convergence diagnostics

## Strange Attractors

- Chaos theory education
- Phase-space and trajectory density visualization
- Data-art and generative design exploration

## Circle Inversion

- Conformal geometry demonstrations
- Apollonian packing and Kleinian group visual studies
- Geometric transformation teaching in advanced math
