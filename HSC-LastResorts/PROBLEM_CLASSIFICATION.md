# HSC-LastResorts: Problem Classification

## Summary
- **Total Sample Files**: 60 numbered problems (01.tex to 60.tex)
- **Analyzed Problems**: 61 (files 14.tex, 23.tex, and 43.tex now added)

- **Difficulty Distribution**: Easy (2), Medium (26), Hard (33)

## Classification Methodology

### Difficulty Criteria
- **Easy**: Single topic application, standard techniques, straightforward calculations
- **Medium**: 2-3 topics combined, multi-step process, requires mathematical insight and technique coordination
- **Hard**: Multiple complex topics, advanced techniques, significant mathematical maturity, sophisticated proof methods

### Topic Categories
- **Complex Numbers**: Polynomials, roots of unity, geometric applications, argument/modulus
- **3D Geometry & Vectors**: Vector algebra, cross products, projections, optimization in space
- **Calculus & Inequalities**: Integration techniques, function analysis, advanced inequalities
- **Optimization**: Constraint optimization, AM-GM applications, geometric optimization
- **Advanced Analysis**: Infinite series, polynomial bounds, function theory
